About Us

Since Year 0, we all have been facing different hardships and its essential to keep humanity alive. Many of us thought that this was the end, but it is never over. Thanks to Bictro Institutes' founding members, we are still alive.

Bictro Institute was established in Year 1 by the founders Yudhisther Lokhande and Kushagr Sharma. After being the leading scientist in the field of human survival, they decided to start a university to help educate the survivors of the disastrous apocalypse. Their research was key in predicting the events that occurred in 2075 preA. The Machine Learning model that they built was helped in evacuations at the correct time.

Ever since Year 1, Bictro Institute has been the leading institute in Education on the planet with up- to-date courses, a real time AI question answering system, and of course the best in-house faculty. The focus of the Institute is to retain as much knowledge as we can from the previous timeline and at the same time progress towards technology 100, the plan that we on earth, could not complete.

Bictro Institute is the oldest institute postA and has continued to be the leading institute in Education for the past 7 years. Ranking number 1 university on Planet Abot for the past 4 years, Bictro Institute scores the most for quality of Education along with student welfare.
